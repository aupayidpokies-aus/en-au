1. Core Principles of Safe Gameplay

Before initiating a deposit to play online pokies, we strongly advise our readers to adhere to the following principles:

  • Set a strict budget: Only gamble with money you can comfortably afford to lose. Never use money allocated for rent, bills, or groceries.
  • Do not chase losses: If you experience a losing session, accept the loss. Attempting to win back lost funds often leads to a destructive cycle.
  • Set time limits: Decide how long you will play before you start, and stick to that limit regardless of whether you are winning or losing.
  • Don't gamble when emotional: Avoid playing when you are stressed, depressed, or under the influence of alcohol or medication, as this impairs judgment.

3. Identifying a Gambling Problem

Problem gambling can develop gradually. It is important to be honest with yourself about your habits. Ask yourself the following questions:

  • Do you hide your gambling activities or the amount you spend from family and friends?
  • Do you feel anxious, irritable, or restless when you try to cut down or stop gambling?
  • Have you borrowed money, sold possessions, or committed illegal acts to fund your gambling?
  • Do you use online pokies as an escape from real-life problems or negative emotions?
  • Are you neglecting work, school, or personal relationships to gamble?

If you answered "yes" to any of these questions, you may be developing a gambling problem and should seek assistance.

5. Blocking Software

If you wish to completely restrict your access to gambling websites across all your devices, we recommend installing specialized blocking software. Programs such as Gamban, Betfilter, or GamBlock can be installed on your computer and smartphone to actively prevent you from accessing online casinos and sportsbooks.

For Australian licensed services, you can also register with BetStop, the National Self-Exclusion Register, which blocks you from all local interactive wagering services.

6. Protection of Minors

It is illegal for anyone under the age of 18 to engage in online gambling. We do not target our content to minors. If you share your computer or mobile device with individuals under the legal gambling age, we strongly advise using parental control software (such as NetNanny or Cyber Patrol) to restrict their access to gambling portals and your PayID banking applications.
